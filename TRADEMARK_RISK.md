# Trademark Risk Review

`i fitness` is a working project label, not a claim of trademark ownership. A preliminary public-web search found existing `IFITNESS` marks and an established China fitness business using the same or highly similar name. This is a collision risk for fitness software, fitness services, and related goods.

This review is not a trademark clearance. Do not use `i fitness` as a commercial or app-store brand until a qualified trademark professional searches the intended jurisdictions and the relevant classes (at minimum software/mobile applications, fitness services, and any paid content or equipment categories) and confirms a filing and use strategy.

For the public source repository, use of the label is descriptive project identification only; no affiliation, endorsement, or licence is implied.
